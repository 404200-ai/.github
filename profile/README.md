# 404200.ai
### Deploying AI for SMEs in Cyprus, EU & MENA

404200.ai is a Cyprus-based AI consultancy that deploys AI for small and medium-sized businesses (SMEs) across Cyprus, the EU, and the MENA region. We help SME owners and founders use AI to save time, reduce costs, and grow — without needing a big tech team. Our mission is to democratise access to frontier technology for the businesses that form the backbone of our economy.

**What we do**
- **AI Readiness Assessments**: Evaluating your current tech stack and identifying the highest-impact opportunities for AI integration.
- **AI Strategy & Roadmapping**: Designing a practical, step-by-step plan to deploy AI across your organisation.
- **Implementation & Workflow Automation**: Building custom AI-powered automations that handle repetitive tasks so your team can focus on what matters.
- **Team Training & Ongoing Support**: Ensuring your people have the skills and support they need to use AI confidently every day.

**Who we work with**
We partner with SME owners, operations managers, founders, and agencies who want to lead their industries rather than play catch-up.
