# Contributing to 404200.ai

Welcome! We are delighted that you're interested in contributing to our open-source resources. At 404200.ai, we believe that sharing knowledge is the best way to help SMEs navigate the AI revolution. Whether you're a developer, a business owner, or an AI enthusiast, your practical insights are invaluable.

### How to contribute

**Suggesting a new tool**
If you've found a tool that has genuinely helped an SME, please suggest it for our `awesome-ai-for-small-business` list. Open an issue or a pull request with the tool name, a brief description of the problem it solves, and why it's a good fit for SMEs.

**Suggesting corrections or new topics**
AI moves fast. If you spot an error or think we've missed a vital topic in any of our READMEs, please let us know. We value accuracy and practical utility above all else.

**Opening an issue**
To help us review your suggestion quickly, please use the following template for issues:
- **Repository**: Which repo does this apply to?
- **Section**: Which section/file needs changing?
- **Change**: What exactly should be changed or added?
- **Why**: Why is this change beneficial for SMEs or our community?

### Our standards
- **Be respectful**: We are a professional community of solvers.
- **No spam**: Please do not use our repos for blatant self-promotion without context.
- **Context is king**: If you're suggesting a tool, explain the *use case*.

All contributions are reviewed by the 404200.ai team to ensure they meet our standards for practical, high-quality advice for the SME community.

Thank you for helping us help others!
